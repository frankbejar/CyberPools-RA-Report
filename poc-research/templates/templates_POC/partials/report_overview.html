Report Overview

How to Interpret Scores

The Overall Risk Score reflects implementation progress across all nine control categories. Category scores show the same metric for just that focus area.

  • 100%: Controls are fully implemented.
  • 90–99%: Minor gaps remain.
  • 75–89%: Meaningful opportunities exist.
  • < 75%: Significant remediation work is needed.

Each risk distribution tile counts the number of controls that fall into high, moderate, or low residual risk bands. The left accent color follows the CyberPools brand palette to draw attention to risk levels.

How to Read Control Cards

Every question tile provides a consistent snapshot of the control status, its importance, and supporting commentary.

Control Question

Numbered per the CyberPools framework. The text explains the required capability.

Control Standard

A concise definition of the expected practice. Use this to understand intent and scope.

Control Status

Bands (Fully, Partially, Not Implemented) show current adoption. Colors follow green / amber / red.

Impact & Risk Score

Impact reflects the criticality of the control. Risk Score = Status value × Impact rating.

Assessment Notes

Field teams capture evidence, context, or next steps. When blank, no additional commentary was provided.
